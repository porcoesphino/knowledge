Waves:

- Primary - P-waves

  - A compression wave, like a sound wave.
  - Fastest in solids > liquids > gases.

- Secondary - S-waves

  - Shear waves, like a guitar string.
  - Can only travel through solids.
  - Don't travel through the outer core, indicating it's liquid
